# Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic

For this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. The infographic explains key concepts such as digital literacy, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

The aim was to present information in a clear and visually appealing way so that students can easily understand the importance of responsible digital usage.

This task helped me understand how visual design can be used to communicate important information effectively.
